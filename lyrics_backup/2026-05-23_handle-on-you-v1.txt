[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I want to pull you close until the shadows bend
We got the moonlight showing where the borders end
Don't need a compass now, I'm letting instinct steer
This is the exact moment I've been craving all year

[Verse 1]
The dashboard glows against the denim on your knees
A gentle shiver riding on the southern breeze
A heavy gravity is pulling from your chest
I know you feel the current, put it to the test

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is a wildfire, heavy in the air
I lose my fingers in the velvet of your hair
The world outside the window doesn't mean a thing
I only want to chase the quiet heat you bring

[Chorus]
I just want to get a handle on you
Feel the rhythm of your heartbeat, steady and true
Let my hands learn the secrets that you try to hide
I want to be the only lover on this midnight ride

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, a handle on you, in the dark, in the glow
Taking our time, keeping it nice and slow

[Verse 2]
You got a whispered promise melting on your lip
I want to trace the edge and navigate the trip
There ain't a highway sign to tell us we should stop
I'm diving in completely, waiting for the drop

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every touch is a wildfire, lighting up the cold
This is the kind of magic that never gets old
So come a little closer, let me hold you right here
Let me pour my devotion softly in your ear

[Chorus]
I just want to get a handle on you
Feel the rhythm of your heartbeat, steady and true
Let my hands learn the secrets that you try to hide
I want to be the only lover on this midnight ride

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, a handle on you, in the dark, in the glow
Taking our time, keeping it nice and slow

[Bridge]
I just want to get a handle, closer to the fire
In the silence and the shadows, caught in the desire
Every echo of the heartbeat leads us to the light
Hold on to the feeling... Saeed in the night
Written in our destiny, we leave the past behind
It's an intimate surrender I was meant to find
[Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)
[Echo] (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
I never knew I could feel something this profound
Just let me stay right here, beautifully bound

[Final Chorus]
I finally got a handle on you
Feeling the rhythm of your heartbeat, steady and true
I'm holding all the secrets that you used to hide
I'm the only lover on this midnight ride
